our school

Our Art School is a leader in online studying. Founded by artist Noah Yoker, our site has been covering the art scene since 2005. Presenting a lively mix of courses,programs, news, reviews and interviews, Art School employs some of the best art teachers, journalists and critics on contemporary art.

At Art School, you’ll master all modern directions as well as classical traditions and be encouraged to let your imaginations take flight. Career artists help perfect techniques in painting, sculpture, jewelry making, and printmaking, while imparting the business savvy necessary to make a living.

Graduates leave the Academy’s fine art school with a portfolio that has collectors and galleries clamoring for their work. You will learn how to find apprenticeships, representatives, and gain exposure in the art world through our expert instructors and the opportunity to showcase your work at exhibits.

We provide relevant approaches to offlain and online learning, internships and employment in the massive companies from all over the word. Additional information about our education system and what do you need for it can be found HERE

what we teach

Education at the Art School begins with the fundamentals. Our curricula focus on the foundations necessary to compose, design, draw, paint, and sculpt. Throughout your academic journey with us, you will develop your artistic point of view as you learn about the artistic and commercial aspects of fine art and create lasting pieces for your student portfolio.

We have four main basic courses: Fine Art, Graphic Design, Graffiti and Illustration. Each of them has many sub-courses (modules) and you can choose a full-fledged basic course as well as its separated modules
