The Art School's Response to COVID-19 (Coronavirus)

The Art School's COVID-19 policies and guidelines are as follows:


  • All individuals, regardless of vaccination status, must wear a mask at all times when in University buildings. Masks may be removed when alone or eating.
  • All individuals, regardless of vaccination status, are required to wear a mask when on Academy buses, shuttles or utilizing other means of University transportation.
  • All individuals are required to self-monitor for symptoms related to COVID-19 prior to coming onsite.
  • All individuals experiencing symptoms of COVID-19 must notify Human Resources immediately.
  • For the safety of everyone, do not come on campus if you are experiencing symptoms of COVID-19. Symptoms may include a fever of 100.4 degrees Fahrenheit or higher, chills, cough, shortness of breath or difﬁculty breathing, fatigue, muscle or body aches, headache, new loss of taste or smell, sore throat, congestion or runny nose, nausea or vomiting, or diarrhea.

What are the Minimum System Requirements for studing?

As an online student at Art School, you will need a personal computer that meets the minimum system requirements to access our Learning Management System. Certain programs and tools may have additional requirements.


Mac


  • Operating System: Mac OS X 10.9 or higher
  • Processor: Intel Core 2 Duo, Core i3, Core i7, or Xeon processor or faster
  • Computer Memory: 4 GB of RAM minimum, 8 GB or more recommended

Windows PC


  • Operating System: Windows 8 or later
  • Processor: 1.33GHz or faster 64-bit processor
  • Computer Memory: 4 GB of RAM minimum, 8 GB or more recommended

Mobile Device (iOS, Android)


  • Most course content is accessible via mobile device
  • Chromebooks are not recommended

What training formats exist?

We offer you the following training formats:


  • Face-to-face groups
  • Individual face-to-face learning
  • Distance learning (online) in group, seminars 3 times a week
  • Individual distance learning (online), seminars 3 times a week
  • Individual distance learning (online) with access to educational information and direct communication with the teacher. No seminars.

Can I change the chosen course or group/format of studying?

Oh,sure. Fill out an application on the website in your account or in the contact section.


Do I need to buy supplies (paints, canvases, clay, plastic, etc.) for classes?

No, everything you need is in abundance at our school. The costs of materials are included in the tuition fee.


How can I pay for tuition?

While studying at the Art school, you can pay for classes:


  • As an individual in the office or online
  • The company for you by bank transfer
  • As part of corporate training.

You can pay for tuition at the Art School of Foreign Languages ​​through the "Settlement" system, as well as through Internet services and e-pay.


How quickly do I have to pay for the lessons?

After the first free lesson, you have 2 days to pay for the tuition.


How to make a return?

The funds paid for tuition are refundable in proportion to the unused part of the tuition fee from the date of termination of the contract

Please note that the refund is made directly to the person who concluded the contract upon presentation of an identity document. Refunds are made exclusively by the method by which payment was made.


How do I add a new question & answer?

To add a new FAQ follow these steps:


  • Click "Manage FAQs" button in your profile
  • From your site's dashboard you can add, edit and manage all your questions and answers
  • Each question and answer should be added to a category
  • Save and publish.
Can I insert an image, video, or gif in my FAQ?

Yes. To add media follow these steps:


  • Click "Manage FAQs" button in your profile
  • Select the question you would like to add media to
  • When editing your answer click on the camera, video, or GIF icon
  • Add media from your library.
How do I edit or remove the "FAQ" title?

You can edit the title from the Settings tab in your profile. If you don't want to display the title, simply disable the Title under "Info to Display".
